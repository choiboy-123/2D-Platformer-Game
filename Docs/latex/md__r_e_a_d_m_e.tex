Use \textquotesingle{}A\textquotesingle{} and \textquotesingle{}D\textquotesingle{} keys to move right and left. Use Space or \textquotesingle{}W\textquotesingle{} key to jump.

Avoid the fire and spiders, collect the coins, and move to the portal to complete the level.
